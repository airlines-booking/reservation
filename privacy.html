Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your personal information

1. Introduction

Trips ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, phone services, or assist with Delta Airlines bookings.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Contact details: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Payment information: Credit/debit card details (processed securely by PCI-compliant partners).
  • Travel information: Passport details, frequent flyer numbers, seat preferences, special assistance requests.
  • Booking details: Flight itineraries, confirmation numbers, and related data needed to complete reservations.

2.2 Technical Data

  • IP address, browser type, operating system.
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referral URLs.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies.

2.3 Call Recordings

Calls to our support line may be recorded for quality assurance, training, and dispute resolution purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage Delta Airlines flight reservations.
  • To communicate booking confirmations, updates, and changes.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Delta Airlines: To complete your booking and manage your reservation.
  • Payment processors: To securely handle transactions.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Service providers: IT support, analytics, and customer experience tools (bound by confidentiality).

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, firewalls, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@trips-delta.com or call +1-505-633-1090.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to Delta Airlines or other sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the bottom indicates when changes were made.
